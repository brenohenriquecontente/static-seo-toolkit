# Audit Checklist

Use this checklist at the end of a task and report only the items that are still missing, broken, or intentionally skipped.

## Per Page

- Title exists and matches the page intent
- Meta description exists and matches visible content
- Canonical exists and points to the correct final URL
- One clear `h1`
- Sensible `h2` and `h3` structure
- Open Graph and Twitter tags present for indexable pages
- Relevant images have useful `alt` text
- Structured data is valid for the visible content

## Site Level

- Internal links expose important pages
- `robots.txt` exists and does not block intended pages
- `sitemap.xml` exists and lists canonical URLs only
- Navigation and footer support crawlability
- Local business details are consistent where applicable

## Reporting Format

- State what was fixed.
- State what could not be confirmed from the provided context.
- State any risks that remain, such as missing final production URLs or missing business details.
