# Local SEO

Use this file when the site represents a clinic, office, restaurant, store, agency, contractor, studio, or any business serving a place-based audience.

## Signals To Add

- Business name
- Primary service or category
- City and state or region
- Address if known
- Phone or WhatsApp if known
- Service area if the business travels to customers
- Opening hours when relevant
- Social profiles when they help trust or discovery

## Where To Place Them

- `<title>` and meta description for the homepage and service pages
- Main headline and subheadline
- Contact block and footer
- FAQ answers about coverage area, booking, or response time
- `LocalBusiness` or a more specific schema subtype

## Writing Rules

- Mention place names naturally. Do not repeat the city in every sentence.
- Match the wording to how customers search: service + location + differentiator.
- If the brief includes multiple neighborhoods or cities, prioritize the main location and mention the others in supporting copy.
- Keep NAP-style data consistent across pages.

## Common Patterns

- Homepage: service + city + differentiator
- Service page: specific offer + city or area served
- Contact page: address, map context, phone, hours, booking CTA
- FAQ: availability, service radius, timeline, pricing approach
